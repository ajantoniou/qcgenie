Video Quality Checker Before YouTube Upload

Quality check videos, podcasts, and clips before you upload.

Run UploadCheck before the final YouTube upload screen so frozen frames, black frames, audio dropouts, caption mistakes, and format issues are caught while your agent or editor can still act.

Run /check workflow View sample report

What the check covers

  • Full-timeline freeze and black-frame checks.
  • Caption safe-area and transcript grounding notes.
  • Audio dropout, clipping, and dead-air evidence.
  • Timestamped findings your agent can cite and route.

Why creators run it before upload

Fewer bad exports

Catch render mistakes before a private, unlisted, or public upload becomes the review copy.

Agent-readable evidence

Use CLI, MCP, REST, or web reports so Claude Code, Codex, and editor workflows see the same timecodes.

Clear fix boundaries

Captions and checklists may be fixable by the agent. Frozen video and bad audio may need a source or render pass.

UploadCheck.app is built for creator teams that need evidence before publishing, not vague post-upload review notes.
